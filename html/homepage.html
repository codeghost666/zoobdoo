eRentPay Portal Features

Property Manager Capabilities:

Post Available Listings

Post your available listings through ERentPay and it will automatically post to other sites site such as

Create, View and Sign Applications & Forms

With everything at your fingertips you can now create, view and have your tenant sign any forms and applications on ERentPay. Everything all in one ERentPay account!

Credit & Background Checks

ERentPay allows you to perform your Background and Credit Checks on any prospective tenant. With ERentPay’s pay-as-you-go service, you pay only for what you use and can manage everything all on your own!

Tenant Capabilities:

Pay Your Rent

Save money when paying your rent online. ERentPay allows for ACH or credit card payments through its automated system. There is also an option for reoccurring payments that save time and money.

Submit Service Requests

Submit Maintenance and Service Requests online to quickly resolve any rental unit issues you may be dealing with. This easy to use system allows you to immediately report any issue and get a response in a timely manner. It also allows for a tracking system of past maintenance issues related to your rental.

Access Rental Documentation

Have complete online access to all Rental documentation you have signed. This allows you to not only view your rental applications and contracts, but also sign them online and check their status quickly.
