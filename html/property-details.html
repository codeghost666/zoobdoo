Chestnut Tower blends urban life with neighborhood warmth. Boasting an amazing city location where the Gold Coast meets River North. Chestnut Tower grants you conveniences such as a smoke free community, 24-hour door staff, 24 hour cyber cafe, 24 hour fitness center, rooftop sun deck and club room. Our most unique amenity is our private fenced in dog park, loved by our Chestnut Pups. Exclusive shopping on Oak St is steps away from you door, as well as many foodie worthy restaurants! Chestnut Tower is a Walkers Paradise with a 100% walkscore. Every apartment features spacious floor plans, in unit washer/dryer and spectacular city views. Chestnut Tower offers new renovated apartments featuring stunning finishes and designer fixtures; including quartz counters in your kitchen and bathroom, glass tile back splash, stainless appliances, wood flooring throughout, usb receptacles and modern cabinets.
