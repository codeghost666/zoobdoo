We actually created ERentPay because we ourselves managed our own properties and wanted a service like this to make it easier and less expensive.

We went down many avenues, including hiring property managers, before deciding to create this service ourselves. We got tired of the high fees, the hassles and the lack of real service. There was nothing out there to really give a landlord or tenant a break. Are you tired of a never ending cycle of people with their hands out trying to get your money without actually earning it? This site puts everything a landlord would need in one place, allowing them to save their money and use their time more efficiently. But this site does more than that; we also wanted to give the landlords knowledge. Here there is no bureaucratic wall to keep what you are missing hidden. You are now free to roam and explore; to effectively manage your time and money and move on to other things in your lives.

When you bought your property you had dreams. There is no reason that you should have to miss out because the management of your property has become a series of reoccurring problems begging for your attention.
